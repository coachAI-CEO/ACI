THE STANDARD YOUR CLUB RUNS ON

Every session built on a
proven 5-phase structure

Every coach on your staff generates sessions with the same scaffolding — guaranteed consistency, every time.

PHASE
1
Warmup
10–15 min
Physical activation, dynamic movement, mental focus. Sets the tone.
PHASE
2
Technical
15–20 min
Skill repetition in low-pressure context. Reinforces fundamentals.
PHASE
3
Tactical
20–25 min
Game model application. Shape, pressing, transitions — your club philosophy.
PHASE
4
Game
15–20 min
Conditioned small-sided game. Constraints that reinforce the session theme.
PHASE
5
Cooldown
5–10 min
Recovery, reflection, coaching conversation. Closes the learning loop.

From match footage
to corrective training plan

Your coaches watch film and know what's wrong. Now they have a tool that tells them exactly how to fix it.

1
Upload a short clip (10–90 sec)

Match footage, training video — any clip where you spotted a problem.

2
Set your context

Age group, player level, coach level — AI adapts its lens accordingly.

3

Critical, Major, Minor — each with confidence scores and timestamps.
