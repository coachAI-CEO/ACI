Setup (5-step organization)
  • Set up a half-pitch area with one full-size goal and two mini-goals on the midline
  • Attackers (6): GK + back four + pivot — in a compact 4-1 shape
  • Defenders (4): Two-plus-two press block — one striker pair, one mid pair
  • Coach stands at edge of D, feeds ball to GK to trigger each rep
  • Rotate defending unit every 2 goals or 4 minutes
Coaching Points
  • DM: open body before ball arrives — see both lines before receiving
  • Fullbacks: time overlap run off DM's first touch forward
  • When pressed wide: pivot must hold central — don't chase ball
  • Striker press: cut back-pass lane first, then press to wide side
Progressions
  • Add second pivot for 7v4 — build overload confidence
  • Flip press trigger direction — test decision-making under new angle
  • Live counter: defenders score in full goal if they win within 6 seconds
  • Remove GK: force ground-only build-up from CB-CB axis
Constraints
  • Must involve pivot before ball crosses midline
  • Max 2 touches in build-up third for advanced
  • No back passes after midline is crossed
Equipment
  • Full-size goal (or two poles)
  • 2 mini-goals on midline
  • 12 bibs (2 colours), 6 cones
  • 4–6 training balls

"Is my first touch going into space — or back into pressure?"

"Am I completing the full half-turn before touching, or just reaching?"
